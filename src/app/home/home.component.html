Student Enrollment


To enroll in a Program

  1. Open the Programs description page by clicking on the Program title.
  2. Click Form.
  3. Choose an option.
  4. Read all instructions and follow any prompts you receive.

Once you are enrolled, you can start taking a course at any time. If you come back to a course after a break, you may need to reset your assessment deadlines.
